Bio

I'm an educator, engineer and musical artist with more than 15 years of professional experience. I got a Bachelor of Science Degree in Communications and Electronics Engineering. Then I majored in Acoustics and got an MBA years later. I've been developing projects for the web for around one year and I really love to create bonds between technology, music and sound.

Currently I teach at Anahuac University and through online courses that I develop. I have experience working with technologies like: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, JSON, AJAX, SCSS, React and P5. I also know about digital audio, MIDI, Processing, PureData, Faust and looking forward to experiment with Web Audio and Web MIDI API's!
