Bio

I'm an engineer, musical artist and educator with more than 15 years of professional experience. I got a Bachelor of Science degree in Communications and Electronics Engineering. Then I majored in Acoustics and got an MBA years later. I've been developing projects for the web for around one year and I'd really love to create bonds between technology, music and sound.

Currently I teach at Anahuac University and through online courses. I have experience working with technologies like: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, JSON, AJAX, SASS, React and Wordpress. I also know about digital audio, MIDI, Processing, PureData, Faust and looking forward to experiment with Web Audio and Web MIDI API's!

On the audio engineering side, I have a lot of experience working with systems like: Pro Tools HD, Logic Pro, Cubase, Ableton Live, Nuendo and with software and hardware processors.
